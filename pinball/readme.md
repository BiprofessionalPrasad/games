✦ To play Mars Zombie Pinball: Outpost Red-Eye, use the following controls:

  ⌨️ Controls
   * Space Bar: Launch the ball (use this to start or if the ball is in the launch lane).
   * 'A' Key: Activate the Left Flipper.
   * 'D' Key: Activate the Right Flipper.

  🚀 Gameplay & Objectives
   * Goal: Keep the ball on the table to increase your Colony Health (score) while surviving the Martian environment.
   * Zombie Bumpers (Green): Hit these glowing cylinders for 100 points and a speed boost.
   * Supply Crates (Orange): These drop from the "sky" every 10 seconds. Smash into them for a massive 500 point bonus.
   * Survival Challenge: Watch the Zombie Hunger meter in the top-left! It increases over time, representing the growing threat to the outpost.

  🛠️ How to Start the Game
   1. In your terminal, run: npm run dev
   2. Click the local link provided (e.g., http://localhost:5173).
   3. Press Space to launch your first ball into the Martian colony!
