# Fairly Land Chase – Game Instructions

## Overview
Welcome to **Fairly Land Chase**, a whimsical endless‑run adventure inspired by a child’s imagination. In this colorful world, a playful unicorn chases you as you sprint through rain‑bows, scoop up ice‑cream cones, and gather cookies. Collect enough treats to earn fake Roblox‑style tokens and claim victory!

---

## Objective
- **Run** as far as you can while avoiding the unicorn.
- **Collect** rainbows, ice‑cream, and cookies to boost your score.
- **Earn** fake Roblox tokens; reaching the target token amount means you **win** the game.

---

## Controls
| Action | Keyboard | Gamepad / Touch |
|--------|----------|-----------------|
| Move Left | ← or **A** | Left Stick / Swipe left |
| Move Right | → or **D** | Right Stick / Swipe right |
| Jump | Spacebar or **W** | **A** button / Tap |
| Slide / Duck | **S** or Down‑Arrow | **B** button / Swipe down |
| Use Special Power‑up (when available) | **E** | **X** button / Double‑tap |

*(Controls can be remapped in the Settings menu.)*

---

## Gameplay Elements
- **Unicorn** – The pursuing creature. If it catches you, the run ends.
- **Rainbow Trails** – Appear randomly; running over them grants a temporary speed boost and points.
- **Ice‑cream Cones** – Collect to gain extra points and a short shield that protects you from one unicorn touch.
- **Cookies** – Gather to fill the “Token Meter”; each cookie adds a small amount toward the win condition.
- **Obstacles** – Low branches, pits, and sticky marshmallows slow you down; avoid or jump over them.
- **Power‑ups** – Occasionally appear (e.g., Sparkle Star) to make the unicorn stumble for a few seconds.

---

## Scoring & Token System
| Item | Points | Token Contribution |
|------|--------|--------------------|
| Rainbow Trail | 10 | +1 Token |
| Ice‑cream Cone | 20 | +2 Tokens |
| Cookie | 15 | +1.5 Tokens (rounded down to nearest whole token when added to meter) |
| Distance Survived (per second) | 5 | — |
| Power‑up Activation | — | — (provides temporary invincibility/slow‑down of unicorn) |

- **Token Meter** displayed at the top‑center of the screen fills as you collect treats.
- **Win Condition**: Reach **100 tokens** (or the target set in the difficulty level). When the meter is full, a victory animation plays, and you receive a congratulatory message with your final fake Roblox token total.

---

## Difficulty Levels
| Level | Unicorn Speed | Token Goal | Notes |
|-------|----------------|------------|-------|
| Easy  | Slow           | 50         | More frequent rainbows & ice‑cream |
| Medium| Normal         | 100        | Balanced gameplay |
| Hard  | Fast           | 150        | Fewer power‑ups, more obstacles |

Select your preferred level before starting a run.

---

## Tips for Success
1. **Stay ahead** of the unicorn by constantly moving sideways; don’t get trapped in corners.
2. **Prioritize rainbows** for speed bursts when the unicorn gets close.
3. **Save ice‑cream cones** for tight spots where you might get hit.
4. **Collect cookies steadily** – they are the main source of token growth.
5. **Use power‑ups wisely**; activating them just before a tight obstacle cluster can save a run.

---

## Ending the Game
- If the unicorn catches you, the run ends and you see your final score, distance, and tokens earned.
- Press **Retry** to start a new run or **Main Menu** to adjust settings, view leaderboards, or quit.
