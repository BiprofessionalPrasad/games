lagori is a Indian villege game from West coast, Maharashtra.
Kids gather around, have a bouncy ball that is used to hit stacked stones (5 or 6 stack) called lagori.
two players play this game, 2nd is computer in this case.
the first player breaks the lagori. so the stones disperse. while the other player grabs the ball 
and tries to hit the player with it while the first player is trying to restack.
if they succeed in restacking they win, if they get hit the other player wins.
